Pool ✈️

Coordinate rides to and from airports with your college classmates.

Save on the price of Ubers to / from airports by sharing rides with your college classmates who have a similar departure date, time, and location.

the steps

  1. enter your departure schedule
  2. get paired with your PoolPal 2+ hours before departure via SMS
  3. finalize details with your PoolPal and order a car together
  4. save money on your trip to the airport. safe travels!

*Pool does not order your ride-share. we provide the contact information of someone who shares your itenerary for you to finalize ride details with.
